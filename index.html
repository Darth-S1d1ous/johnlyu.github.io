Biography

Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Lyu Wenzhuang (in Chinese 吕文壮). My English name is John. I am a sophomore from The University of Hong Kong, majoring in Computer Science. I have participated in the Robomaster competition as radar station leader for 2 years and developed strong interests in computer vision and Deep Learning. In early 2025, I worked as a research assistant in InfoBodied Lab and is resonsible for writing camera driver and conduct experiments to collect and evaluate data. Currently, I am focusing on Computer Graphics and Web Development. New things and technology always fascinates me, and I will never hesitate to give them a try. If there are any potential collaborations or opportunities, please feel free to contact me.
